About

Chandana Chirumamilla

I'm currently studying AI and machine learning at Indiana Wesleyan University. Along with the technical side, my program pushes me to actually reflect on what I'm learning and think about my own growth, not just memorize concepts for a test.

What I enjoy most is figuring out how to explain technical ideas in a way that makes sense to someone who isn't deep in the field. I think that's just as valuable as knowing the math behind a model. So this portfolio isn't just a collection of assignments — it's me taking what I learn in class and reworking it so it actually means something to someone outside the classroom.

I'm still early in this journey, and I'm okay saying that out loud instead of pretending I've got it all figured out. Think of this site as a work in progress that grows as I do — I'll keep adding to it as I finish new projects. If you're a recruiter, a fellow student, or just someone curious about AI, I'd love to hear what you think.

Why this portfolio

Learning in public, on purpose

Every artifact here started as a class assignment, then went through a second pass: rewritten and reframed for whoever might actually read it outside a classroom — a recruiter, a collaborator, or someone curious about the field. That reframing is its own skill, and it's one I'm practicing deliberately, one artifact at a time.
